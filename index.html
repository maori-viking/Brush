Brush

Brush all began in 2000. Over the years, we have maintained a professional edge with our focus being on healthy hair with a difference. Our philosophy on hair is to deliver what the client wants. We listen to you and our desire is to achieve great results, whether it be in cutting, colouring, foiling, keratin treatments and home care.

We keep abreast of the industry by doing courses every year. We use and recommend Kevin.Murphy and Revlon professional for our professional retail and our colours are Revlonissimo from Italy. We are now a Kevin.Murphy session salon. His training and knowledge has helped keep us ahead of our industry, as well as helping build a good relationship with him and our clients.

We hope you take the time to browse our website to find out what we can offer you and we look forward to meeting you in our salon.
